Frequently Asked Questions

What types of courses are available?

We offer a variety of courses, including online, offline, short-term, and long-term programs in fields such as technology, business, and personal development.

Will I get a certificate after completion?

Yes. After successfully completing the course requirements, you will receive a digital certificate that you can download.

Can I get a refund if I’m not satisfied?

Refund policies vary by course. Please check the refund/return policy mentioned during enrollment.

Who do I contact for support?

For any queries, you can email our support team at support@example.com or use the live chat option on our website.

What study materials will I get?

You’ll receive access to video lectures, eBooks, assignments, quizzes, and sometimes project files or case studies

What types of courses are available?

We offer a variety of courses, including online, offline, short-term, and long-term programs in fields such as technology, business, and personal development.

Do I need specific software or tools?

Some technical courses may require software (like Python, Photoshop, MS Excel). Instructions and installation guides are provided.

Will I have lifetime access?

Most courses provide lifetime access to study materials. However, live session recordings may be available only for a limited period (e.g., 6–12 months).

How do assessments work?

Assessments include quizzes, assignments, projects, or exams depending on the course. Some courses also include peer reviews.

Do you offer career support?

Yes. Selected professional programs include resume reviews, mock interviews, and job placement assistance.
